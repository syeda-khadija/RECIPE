Russian Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 potatoes, boiled and diced
  • 2 carrots, boiled and diced
  • 1 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, carrots, and peas.
  2. Add the mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly coated.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
